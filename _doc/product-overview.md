# Product Overview

## What It Is
A web-based image and video editing application designed for global use — a single, unified platform where creators, communicators, and media producers across all nations can edit both images and video together, without friction.

## Users
- **Content creators** — social media producers, bloggers, and independent creatives worldwide
- **Marketers and communicators** — professionals who produce and adapt visual content for diverse audiences
- **Individuals and small teams** — anyone, in any country, who wants quality media editing without juggling multiple specialised tools

## Core Problem
Creators worldwide are forced to use fragmented, often region-specific tools — one app for images, another for video, rarely designed with a global audience in mind. The result: wasted time, inconsistent quality, and a barrier that keeps many creators from making work they're proud of.

## The Solution
A unified editing platform — images and video in a single, coherent experience — built global-first so any creator, in any nation, can produce professional-quality media without friction or tool-switching.

## Core Scope
- **Image editing**: crop, resize, filters, color adjustment, text overlays, compositions, multi-format export
- **Video editing**: trim, merge, timeline editing, effects, transitions, subtitle and text overlay, multi-format export
- **Effects & visual tools**: rich library of filters, effects, and overlays for both images and video
- **Text & captions**: styled text, captions, and subtitles on any project
- **Export & share**: multi-format export; share-ready output from one place

## Global Positioning
Global accessibility is a first-class design principle: the platform is built to serve creators regardless of their nation, language context, or cultural background.

## Brand & Tone
- **Inclusive** — welcoming to creators of all backgrounds, nations, and skill levels
- **Empowering** — makes capable editing achievable for anyone
- **Calm and professional** — reliable, focused, and never overwhelming

## Strategic Principles
1. **Global-first**: the needs of creators across all nations drive design decisions
2. **Unified**: one coherent editing experience for both images and video
3. **Satisfaction-led**: the primary success metric is whether creators everywhere are genuinely productive and satisfied
